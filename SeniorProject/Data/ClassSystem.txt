Characters start by choosing their class

	What stats do I want? 
	Intelligence affects how fast you gain XP
	Speed affects how quickly runes fall? 
	Health (how much damage can take before death)
	Magic (affects how many actions you can take before recharging)
	Stamina for non-magical attacks?  
	Armor - affects how much damage you take from regular attacks 
	Magical Armor - affects how much damage you take from magical attacks
